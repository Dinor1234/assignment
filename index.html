Biography

A Journey of Possibilities

Born in Lagos, Nigeria to the family of Mr and Mrs Obot, Andino is the 4th of 5 children. He attended Christ the Saviour Primary school and Renics Secondary school in Lagos before moving to University of Uyo, Uyo for his university degree in 2013. He graduated with a Bachelors degree in Communication Arts in 2020 after which he served in Maxwell Khobe Cantonment, Bassa, Plateau State. Andino Obot is a customer service professional who is passionate about helping people solve problems. Andino started his career as a brand merchandizer for Sollatek Nigeria. He was in this role for a year before moving to Renmoney Microfinance bank as a User Success Advisor in 2022. He has served as a relationship manager, a customer service representative, and a sales agent.
